This is the wireframes for the website. For this website, there is one first level page, which is the homepage. After that, there are 5 second level pages: about page, community page, login page, profile page, and critique page. The about page is just a popup that introduces the website and tips and tricks on how to critique. The community page will be the page where everyone can see everyone's work. The login page is where people will log in. The profile page will be what the user will see when they're in their account. The critique page is the editing page where users can annotate and comment on someone else's work.
