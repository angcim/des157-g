CRITIQUE REQUIREMENTS

In order for designers to improve their works and portfolios for showcase, they must receive thoughtful criticism and feedback.

HOW TO GIVE PROPER CRITIQUES

1) Discuss about strengths and weaknesses. (don’t just say that you like it or not, or if it’s good or bad, elaborate on why it’s effective/not effective)

2) Don’t try to solve the problem. Let the designer find the solution. (example: don’t say change the font from one to another due to legibility issues, but say the font is hard to read and let the designer decide on how to fix it)
