Storyboards

For my storyboards I created two scenarios on how the website will be used. The first one is the user uploading their work to get critiques. It shows the process of uploading their work such as logging in, uploading, writing descriptions, and submitting. The second scenario is a user critiquing. The user must first log in and then look at other's designers work. Once they chose a project they want to critique, they press edit and have the ability to annotate and comment on the image. After everything is complete, press submit and the process repeats.
